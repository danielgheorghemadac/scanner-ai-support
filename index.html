Frequently asked questions

What does Scanner AI do?

Capture a photo or import any file — documents, PDFs, Office files, receipts, even 3D/CAD files — and Scanner AI reads it, gives you a clean summary, the key details, and one-tap actions (add to calendar, search, copy, share, save).

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs on your device first, and your scans are stored only on your iPhone. AI analysis is optional and only runs when you turn it on — and even then only the extracted text is sent for analysis, never your original file. No accounts, no tracking, no ads. See our Privacy Policy.

How do subscriptions work?

Scanner AI is free to use. Optional monthly plans (Color, Gradient, Premium, Ultimate) unlock sharper AI, icon themes, and more. Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID and renew automatically until cancelled.

How do I cancel a subscription?

On your iPhone: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Scanner AI → Cancel. You keep access until the end of the current billing period.

What file types can it read?

Photos and images, PDFs, Office documents, plain text/data, and common 3D/CAD formats (STL, STEP, OBJ, DXF, 3MF, and more).
