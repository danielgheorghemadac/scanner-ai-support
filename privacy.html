Privacy Policy

Scanner AI · Effective 10 June 2026

Scanner AI is built to be private by design. This policy explains, in plain terms, what the app does and does not do with your information.

The short version

  • Your scans and files stay on your device. We don't upload or store them.
  • We have no accounts, run no ads, and use no third-party tracking or analytics.
  • AI analysis is optional and only runs after you turn it on.

What stays on your device

Photos, files, and scans you capture or import — and the results Scanner AI generates — are stored locally on your iPhone (your "Vault"). They are never sent to us, and we have no server that holds your documents.

Optional AI analysis

If you choose to enable AI analysis, the app extracts text from your document on your device and sends only that text — never the original file or image — over an encrypted connection to a third-party AI provider (such as Google Gemini or DeepSeek), via a relay we operate that hides our API keys. The provider returns a summary, which is shown to you. We do not store this text on our side, and you can decline AI at first launch or leave the app fully on-device.

Subscriptions & payments

Optional subscriptions are processed by Apple through your Apple ID. We never see or receive your payment details. Apple's privacy policy governs that transaction.

Permissions

The app may ask for Camera access (to scan) and access to files/photos you choose to import. These are used only to perform the scan you requested and are not collected by us.

Children

Scanner AI is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the effective date above.

Questions? Email our support address, or see the Support page.
